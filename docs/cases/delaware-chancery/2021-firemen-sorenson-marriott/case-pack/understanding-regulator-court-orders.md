# Understanding Regulatory and Court Orders (Firemen’s — Marriott / Delaware Chancery)

## Purpose

Orient readers to the **Delaware Court of Chancery** decision in **derivative** litigation concerning **Marriott**’s **Starwood** reservation database **breach** and **board oversight** themes.

---

## 1. Chancery opinion

**Firemen’s Retirement System of St. Louis v. Sorenson**, C.A. No. 2019-0965-LWW (Del. Ch.).

- **Delaware courts:** [Opinion download](https://courts.delaware.gov/Opinions/Download.aspx?id=325170)

### What to read for

The opinion addresses **derivative** **pleading** and **demand futility** in the context of **cybersecurity** **oversight** and **M&A** **diligence** narratives surrounding the **Starwood** breach. Read the opinion for specific holdings and the claims that survived or failed.
