# Controls -> Evidence Map (Yahoo MDL (2018))

> Technical appendix for leadership and compliance.

## Purpose

This technical appendix maps controls to objective evidence for Controls -> Evidence Map (Yahoo MDL (2018)), enabling rapid substantiation of implementation and operating effectiveness. It is used by security, compliance, and legal teams to demonstrate what is deployed, how it is monitored, and what records support examiner or litigation requests.

<p><strong>Technical Objective: </strong>This appendix maps required control states to objective evidence for the Yahoo customer data breach MDL standing and class-litigation context. It is intended for legal, compliance, and security review where implementation proof is required.</p>

<p><strong>1) Governance and Accountability: </strong><br>
<strong>Required Control State: </strong>Documented ownership, escalation pathways, and periodic executive review for in-scope security and disclosure risks.<br>
<strong>Evidence Artifacts: </strong>Committee minutes, governance charters, risk-acceptance approvals, and remediation tracker exports.<br>
<strong>Verification Signals: </strong>On-time review cadence, escalation SLA adherence, and aging of high-risk open items.</p>

<p><strong>2) Control Implementation and Monitoring: </strong><br>
<strong>Required Control State: </strong>Preventive and detective controls are implemented for identity, configuration, data access, and incident response paths relevant to the case posture.<br>
<strong>Evidence Artifacts: </strong>Configuration baselines, access-review attestations, SIEM rule catalogs, and incident investigation tickets.<br>
<strong>Verification Signals: </strong>Coverage percentages, mean time to detect and respond, and exception closure rates.</p>

<p><strong>3) Legal and Regulatory Readiness: </strong><br>
<strong>Required Control State: </strong>Records and logs are retained in a retrievable format aligned to examiner, regulator, or litigation requests.<br>
<strong>Evidence Artifacts: </strong>Retention policies, evidence indexes, legal-hold records, and third-party assessment outputs.<br>
<strong>Verification Signals: </strong>Request turnaround time, completeness scores for evidence packages, and reduction in repeat findings.</p>

<p><strong>Submission Note: </strong>This map is technical by design and intended to accompany executive or regulator-facing narratives.</p>
