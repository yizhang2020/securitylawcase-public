## 1. FTC stipulated order (2019)

### Official document

**Stipulated order** — *In the Matter of Equifax Inc.*, FTC (filed **July 22, 2019**).

- **PDF:** [FTC Equifax order (signed)](https://www.ftc.gov/system/files/documents/cases/equifax/172_3203_equifax_order_signed_7-23-19.pdf)

The order (read the PDF) imposes a comprehensive **information security program**, **assessments**, and **consumer redress** obligations, among other terms.
