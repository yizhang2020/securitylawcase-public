## Purpose

Track the **SEC’s** **civil enforcement** action against **SolarWinds** and its **CISO** (as filed), the **district court’s** **motion to dismiss** ruling, and the **2025** **stipulation** **dismissing** the case **with prejudice**.

---

## 1. SEC complaint

**SEC v. SolarWinds Corp. and Timothy G. Brown**, No. **1:23-cv-09518** (S.D.N.Y.), filed **Oct. 30, 2023**.

- **Complaint PDF:** [comp-pr2023-227.pdf](https://www.sec.gov/files/litigation/complaints/2023/comp-pr2023-227.pdf)
- **Press release:** [2023-227](https://www.sec.gov/newsroom/press-releases/2023-227)

The complaint alleges **fraud** and **internal control** failures relating to **cybersecurity** **representations** and the **SUNBURST** period. Read the complaint for specific theories.

---

## 2. District court motion to dismiss decision

The court issued a **decision** on defendants’ **motion to dismiss** reported at **741 F. Supp. 3d 37** (**July 18, 2024**). Read the opinion for which claims survived or were dismissed at that stage.

---

## 3. Dismissal stipulation (2025)

On **November 20, 2025**, the **SEC** announced a **joint stipulation** **dismissing** the **enforcement** action **with prejudice**. See **Litigation Release** **LR-26423** and the filed stipulation.

- **LR-26423:** [SEC litigation release](https://www.sec.gov/enforcement-litigation/litigation-releases/lr-26423)
