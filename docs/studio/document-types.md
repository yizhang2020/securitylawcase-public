## How to use this catalog

1. **Match your trigger** — Use [Workflows](workflows.md) to see which document types apply to your situation.  
2. **Open the reference** — Follow the link to the detailed page for structure, audience, and tips.  
3. **Draft** — Use the structure and guidance; in premium, use templates and generation support.  
4. **Review** — Align with evidence and primary sources; get appropriate sign-off.

All document types are designed to support **evidence readiness** and **defensibility**—so what you produce can be traced to controls and facts that regulators and counsel expect.
